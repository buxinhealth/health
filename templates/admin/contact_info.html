How to get the embed URL:
1. Go to Google Maps
2. Search for your location or click on the place
3. Click the "Share" button
4. Select the "Embed a map" tab
5. Copy the entire src="..." URL from the iframe code

Example format:
https://www.google.com/maps/embed?pb=!1m18!1m12!1m3!1d...!2m3!1f0!2f0!3f0!3m2!1i1024!2i768!4f13.1...

⚠️ Important: You cannot use regular Google Maps place URLs (like the one you tried). You must use the embed URL from the "Embed a map" option.
